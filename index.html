Hey there, I'm Tyson.

As a young man, I worked very blue collar jobs for local small businesses while discovering my passion for computers & programming. After a lot of work and too many long days to count, I’m now a Brand Designer, Front-End Developer, and a WordPress Specialist. My day to day work is basically helping small businesses help their owners by getting more attention to their company.

Having been born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, I like to spend my free time outdoors, rain or shine. When I’m not hard at work, I like to mountain bike, snowboard, backpack, or west coast swing. With my sidekick-husky, Hank, you can check out all our adventure movies via YouTube and Insta!
